About John's Walang Logtu Cafe

John's Walang Logtu Cafe was founded to be a friendly hangout in BF Resort Village, Las Piñas, where everyone can relax and connect. Motivated by a love for coffee and people, we opened our doors to provide not just beverages, but memorable moments. Every drink is made with attention, quality, and a sense of belonging.
