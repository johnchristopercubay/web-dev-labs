Colorful Filipino Dessert

Halo-Halo is a famous Filipino dessert perfect for hot weather. It is a mix of shaved ice, evaporated milk, sweetened beans, fruits, jellies, and topped with leche flan or ube ice cream. The name "Halo-Halo" literally means "mix-mix," encouraging everyone to stir all the ingredients together before eating. In Las Piñas, halo-halo stalls are popular during the summer season, offering colorful and refreshing variations of this sweet treat.
