Crispy and Flavorful

Lechon is one of the most iconic Filipino dishes. It is a whole pig roasted over charcoal, giving it a crispy golden-brown skin and juicy, flavorful meat. In Las Piñas, lechon is a centerpiece in fiestas and special occasions. The crispy skin paired with liver sauce makes this dish unforgettable, and a true celebration is never complete without it.
