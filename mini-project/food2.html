A Classic Noodle Dish

Pancit Malabon is a noodle dish that originated in Malabon, but it has also become a favorite in Las Piñas. It is made with thick rice noodles, a rich shrimp-based sauce, and topped with seafood like shrimp, squid, and smoked fish, along with sliced eggs and chicharon. This dish is often served during birthdays and gatherings, symbolizing long life and prosperity.
