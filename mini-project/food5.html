Sweet Rice Cake Delight

Bibingka is a traditional Filipino rice cake usually enjoyed during the Christmas season. It is baked in clay pots lined with banana leaves, giving it a distinct aroma and flavor. Topped with salted egg, cheese, and sometimes grated coconut, bibingka is a sweet and savory treat that pairs well with hot tea or coffee. In Las Piñas, local bakeries and street vendors sell freshly made bibingka, especially near churches during Simbang Gabi.
